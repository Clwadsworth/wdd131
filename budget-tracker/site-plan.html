Overview

Purpose

This app exists to give the individual a bird's-eye view of where money is actually going instead of guessing after the expense hits the bank account. It's not only about seeing the numbers, but it's about building enough structure and perspective that anyone can plan ahead and build healthier habits instead of just reacting to whatever's left in the account.

Audience

My audience is for those who struggle with keeping track of how they're spending or what expenses they're paying for, whether they are large or small.

Dynamic elements

Multiple button click events. One will do a pop-up into a form modal that will allow different interactions by selecting an item and modifying it. Another button click event will then select values, such as labels, category labels which include categories, amount, and where it is spent, and builds out new objects and pushes those objects into our arrays. Those arrays will then, through a calculation, sum up each category and produce a total that can be displayed to the user. The actual else if is something that will review it based on the array from the budget limit in the first place and run it through a function that will verify if the total expenditure array exceeds the budget limit that's already placed. It would then produce an event that will produce a pop-up saying that it will acknowledge that the budget has been overspent.
